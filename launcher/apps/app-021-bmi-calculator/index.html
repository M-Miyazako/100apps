About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's a useful screening tool, but it doesn't directly measure body fat percentage.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 and above

Important Notes

  • BMI may not be accurate for athletes with high muscle mass
  • Age, gender, and body composition affect interpretation
  • Consult healthcare professionals for personalized advice
  • BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure
