Validation Rules

  • Name: Must be at least 2 characters, letters only
  • Email: Must be a valid email format
  • Phone: Must be a valid US phone number format
  • Password: At least 8 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
  • Credit Card: Must be a valid card number (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Expiry Date: Must be MM/YY format and not expired
  • CVV: 3-4 digits depending on card type
  • Age: Must be between 13 and 120
  • Website: Must be a valid URL
  • Username: 3-20 characters, alphanumeric and underscores only
